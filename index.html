Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Glass Express ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Glass Express mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information from users of the App.

The App may request access to your device's internet connection to display web content. No personal data is transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

The App doesn't use third-party services.

Log Data

The App itself does not collect log data. However, third-party services may collect information such as your device's IP address, device name, operating system version, and configuration when you use their services.

Cookies

The App does not use cookies. However, websites loaded within the App's webview may use cookies as described in their respective privacy policies.

Security

We value your trust in using our App. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Links to Other Sites

The App may contain links to external sites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. We strongly advise you to review the privacy policy of these websites.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us
